Question 1 : Write a short paragraph in your own words defining what a 'program' is and give one real-life example from any app you use daily (like Instagram, Zomato, or Paytm).
Answer: A program is a set of instructions that tells a computer what to do to complete a specific task. It works in the background and helps apps perform different functions without us noticing. For example, when I use Instagram, the program loads my feed, shows new posts, and lets me like or comment on photos.

Question 4 : Write down the steps (as an algorithm, using simple numbered instructions) for ordering food on Swiggy — from opening the app to completing payment.
Answer: 1. Open the Swiggy app on your phone.
        2. Enter or confirm your delivery location.
        3. Search for the restaurant or food you want.
        4. Select the dishes and add them to your cart.
        5. Check your order and change the quantity if needed.
        6. Tap **Proceed to Checkout**.
        7. Choose your delivery address and payment method.
        8. Complete the payment.
        9. Wait for the order confirmation and track your delivery.
